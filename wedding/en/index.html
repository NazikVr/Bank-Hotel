ART CONGRESS HALL - an elegant location in the city center, with an area of 500 square meters, which comfortably accommodates 110 guests of your holiday. The atmosphere of elegant classics harmonizes well with modern technologies, the large space provides an opportunity to equip the hall with a stage, music and lighting equipment, experiment with the placement of tables - there is enough space for both a banquet and dancing!

And:

  • professional performance stage
  • modern sound and light equipment
  • all possible guest accommodation options
  • lounge area
  • dressing rooms

SAFE Restaurant is a refined location for happy moments. The SAFE restaurant accommodates 60 guests.

The unique interior of the SAFE restaurant hall, the open plastering of the original building of the Austro -Hungarian Bank, the Austrian chandeliers and individual zones for Bar Candy and Dancing - add unforgettable experiences.

Wine Bar with a terrace is a real hotel decoration and a perfect location for chamber celebrations.

The Wine Bar wine room accommodates up to 20 people. The wine room with a terrace on the seventh floor is perfect for cozy, chamber celebrations. The unsurpassed interior, fireplace, unforgettable panorama to the old town, and most importantly, complete isolation from the rest of the hotel.
